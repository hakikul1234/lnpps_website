School Facilities

Modern Infrastructure & Facilities

Our school is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities designed to support quality education and overall student development.

Playground

Spacious, well-maintained playground for sports and physical activities. Dedicated areas for various sports including cricket, badminton, and running track.

📚

Library

Well-stocked library with a comprehensive collection of books, reference materials, and reading resources. Quiet reading areas and digital resources available.

💻

Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with latest technology and high-speed internet. Equipped for teaching computer science, coding, and digital literacy skills.

🚰

Toilet Facilities

Clean, hygienic, and well-maintained toilet facilities for boys and girls. Separate facilities for staff and students with regular maintenance and sanitization.

Clean Classrooms

Spacious, bright, and well-ventilated classrooms with proper lighting. Regularly cleaned and maintained with comfortable seating and modern teaching aids.

Safe and reliable transportation facility available for students from nearby areas. School buses are well-maintained and operated with experienced drivers to ensure comfortable and secure travel.
