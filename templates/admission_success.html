Frequently Asked Questions

When will I hear back about my application?

You will receive a confirmation call within 3-5 business days. We process applications in the order they are received.

What documents do I need to bring?

Birth Certificate, Aadhar Card, Address Proof, Passport photos, and previous school certificates (if applicable).

Is there an admission fee?

No, admission is completely FREE! You only pay the examination fee (₹1000/year) and monthly tuition fees.

Can I change the class after admission?

Class changes are generally not allowed unless approved by the school management based on specific circumstances.

What is the refund policy?

Fees are non-refundable. However, contact the office for special circumstances or withdrawal policies.

How do I pay the fees?

Fees can be paid monthly via cash or online transfer. Contact our office for payment details and methods.
